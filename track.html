    Where the effort goes

    Your receptive skills — reading especially — are nearly there and will reach B2 with little extra; that's your translator's edge. The whole game is production. Speaking is the floor, and all-around B2 is measured by it. So weight your hours toward output and, crucially, live conversation — a tutor or exchange, not just self-talk. That single bar decides whether all-around B2 lands in December 2027 or a little past it. Updating these levels also moves the roadmap's overall position.
